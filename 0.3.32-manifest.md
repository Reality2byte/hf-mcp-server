### 0.3.32 Spaces result

Status: **PASS for the server, with an iteration-limit caveat**. Only the current `files` variant was run.

Opus completed all 16 rows and supplied `mcp-server` evidence in all six MCP-required answers. The original GPT-family
card allowed only eight iterations. GPT-5.6 made only successful tool calls but exhausted that ceiling while continuing
to inspect evidence on three rows. Re-running exactly those three prompts with 12 iterations produced three non-empty
answers; both MCP-required answers included explicit MCP/tag or `mcp_server=True` evidence. This establishes that the
empty outputs were a harness cutoff rather than empty semantic-search results.

GPT-5.4-mini is retained as a non-gating diagnostic because it had no historical Space baseline. At eight iterations it
also produced three empty final answers and several malformed calls, despite obtaining relevant results. Future Space
matrices should use `max_iterations: 12`, consistent with the docs card.

|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|

| GPT-5.6, max 8 | 81.3% | 100% | 3/16 | 4/6 | harness ceiling |
